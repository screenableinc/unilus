Bachelor of Science in Human Resource Management (HRM)

Course over view

Duration

The course of study of the Bachelor of Science in Human Resource Management degree shall be completed in a period of:
(i) Four (4) years for Full-Time ,Part-Time and Long Distance Students.

Entry requirements

Minimum entry requirement: Five (5) “O” level credits or better which must include English Language and Mathematics.
